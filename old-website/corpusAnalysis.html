Conspiracies vs. Political Texts

As long as there is a government, people will question the powers that be. With the immergence of the Internet, people questioning the government and its antics is now fairly common. Any one can now access documents that (allegedly) reveal all of big brother's darkest secrets through online forums and databases. TextFiles.com is one website that has archived countless documents related to conspiracies and political files.

I picked 23 random files each from the Conspiracy and Political directories, and compared the two categories.
