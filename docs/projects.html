OVERDOSE

Overdose is a video game project that I've been conceiving for over four years. I was fortunate enough to work on it as a research project in my junior year of college.

Overdose is intended to be a rogue-lite survival game that depicts the consequences of drug use, by implementing it as the primary mechanic. The player is expected to manage their addictive tendencies without dying from external threats or the overuse of psychoactive substances.

The game is currently not out yet, however you can follow its development on this YouTube channel.
